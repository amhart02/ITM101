Why Elephants Are Amazing

Elephants are known for their intelligence, strong social bonds, and incredible memory. They live in close family groups and communicate using sounds, body language, and even vibrations through the ground.

Fun Facts

  • Elephants can weigh up to 14,000 pounds.
  • They use their trunks for breathing, drinking, and grabbing food.
  • Elephants have excellent long-term memory.
